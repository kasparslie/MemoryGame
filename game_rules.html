Memory Game Rules

At the beginning of the game, all cards are mixed up and placed face down on the table and a timer will start.

The player starts and turns over two cards:

- If the cards don’t match, the cards will be flipped back again.

- if the cards do match, it’s a pair! The player earns a point.

When the player have found all the pairs before the time has run out the player wins!
